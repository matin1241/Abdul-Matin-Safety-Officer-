I am Abdul Matin, a dedicated and Aramco-approved HSE Officer with over 5 years of front-line experience across some of the most demanding industrial environments in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia — from offshore platforms in Berri to high-pressure gas compression facilities in the Jafurah field.

My approach to safety goes beyond regulatory compliance. I believe a true safety culture is built through consistent leadership on the ground, meaningful communication with every worker, and a genuine commitment to sending every team member home safely at the end of each shift.

Equipped with internationally recognised qualifications — NEBOSH IGC (UK), IOSH Managing Safely, and OSHA-30 — I bring a rigorous, risk-based methodology to every project I join, continuously raising the bar for what safety excellence looks like.
